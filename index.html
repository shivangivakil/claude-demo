01 — About

Orchestrating excellence
across people & operations.

Shivangi Vakil is a strategic advisor and consultant with 20+ years of executive leadership spanning human capital, finance, legal, compliance, and operational strategy — most recently as CHRO & Head of RMG at Gateway Group of Companies and formerly COO of AutoFacets.

Today she partners with founders, CEOs, and boards to design the systems that turn early traction into durable scale: talent architecture, financial governance, operating rhythms, and the cultural foundations that hold everything together.

Her work is anchored in a simple conviction: organizations grow fastest when their people are aligned, empowered, and supported by operational systems built to scale with ambition. She holds an MBA specialized in Finance and Human Resources and is based in Ahmedabad, India.
